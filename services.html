Our Service

Counselling

WATTC supports widows who face various challenges by providing free counselling for them in order to look out for their well-being

Sustainable Development Projects

WATTC runs projects that support sustainability of its operations and its members. The project ranges from commercial agriculture practices to business ventures such as Green House Agriculture, Cake Baking and tent hiring

Table Banking

WATTC offers small business and provides loans to small widows across Taita Taveta County at affordable rates to support their growth and development.
