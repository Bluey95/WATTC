Foundation

WATTC was found in 2013 by a group of widows, Christine Senja, Hope Wawuda and Hon. Joyce Lay. From the result of facing simailar challenges of emotional distress, poor eceonomic well being and social rejection, they saw the need of coming together for a common purpose of sustaining their families through joint effort in development of projects. They formed small subgroups across the four constituencies in Taita taveta County which form the umbrella group, The WATTC.

Growth

Since its inception, more subgroups in the constituency levels have registered and joined the organization. With the growing numbers, we have been able to run several projects and give loans to our members. Our main aim is to counsel, run income generating projects to sustain their lives and provide table banking to support their economic growth.
